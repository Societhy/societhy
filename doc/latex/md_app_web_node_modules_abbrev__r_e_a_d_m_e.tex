Just like \href{http://apidock.com/ruby/Abbrev}{\tt ruby\textquotesingle{}s Abbrev}.

Usage\+: \begin{DoxyVerb}var abbrev = require("abbrev");
abbrev("foo", "fool", "folding", "flop");

// returns:
{ fl: 'flop'
, flo: 'flop'
, flop: 'flop'
, fol: 'folding'
, fold: 'folding'
, foldi: 'folding'
, foldin: 'folding'
, folding: 'folding'
, foo: 'foo'
, fool: 'fool'
}
\end{DoxyVerb}


This is handy for command-\/line scripts, or other cases where you want to be able to accept shorthands.
